Paella Recipe

Description:

Dish consisting of an onion and tomato 'sofrito', vegetables, seafood and rice cooked in a fish stock

Ingredients:

  • Sofrito
  • Vegetables
  • Seafood
  • Rice (short-grain)
  • Fish Stock

Steps:

  1. Cook the seafood over medium high heat with abundant olive oil until caramelized
  2. Add vegetables and the previously cooked sofrito
  3. Add the stock and bring to a simmer
  4. Add the rice
  5. Cook on medium high heat for 10 minutes, reduce to medium for 5 minutes and let rest with the gas off for another 5 minutes
